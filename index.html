Internet (technology)

Why did the internet become even more important during this period?

Obviously, humanity has already faced several pandemics, but this one has gained stratospheric strength due to its propagation power. In addition to the power of the media, which has been divulging issues related to COVID-19 all the time.

Before the emergence of the coronavirus in the country, we met our friends and family more often. Hugs and kisses, for example, were natural. It was part of people's routine to go to restaurants, beaches and shopping centers. However, everything changed with the emergence of COVID-19.

Fear and precautions physically distanced people. Some environments began to be avoided. In this way, the use of technology and the internet became essential.

The technology has certainly helped us a lot. If not for technology, our situation would be much worse, both in depression and in psychic isolation. The affection can be transmitted through messages, calls and video calls, even if not with the same fullness”, says João Paulo Branco, psychiatrist and psychogeriatrician....

 .Pandemic

What would we be without the internet during the pandemic?

The internet originated in 1969 as a military and scientific resource. Therefore, it benefited people a lot, giving easy access to knowledge, social inclusion, as a work tool and also for fun. Thus, the internet is recognized as an instrument of fundamental importance to society.

During the pandemic, the internet has enabled essential services: schools with distance learning, commercial movements, home office work, communication, the financial sector, lives being broadcast by artists to replace shows, among other things.

The coronavirus has become a "watershed", bringing this new form of life. The world before and after COVID-19 will go down in history. Many changes were made to keep the economy minimally active during the pandemic, for example, we had to adapt to a new lifestyle. The internet, in turn, also plays a key role in this change.
